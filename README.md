### 👤 About Me

I'm a Data Scientist focused on **credit risk modeling** and **MLOps**, currently building internal scoring and analytics tools in the financial services industry. My day-to-day sits right where data engineering, statistical modeling, and production ML meet: I build the pipelines that move and shape the data, develop the models on top of it, and then do the engineering work to get those models running reliably in production.

I care less about one-off notebooks and more about systems that keep working — reproducible experiments, automated retraining, and models whose decisions can be explained and audited. That mindset comes naturally from the credit domain, where a model's *transparency* matters as much as its accuracy.
